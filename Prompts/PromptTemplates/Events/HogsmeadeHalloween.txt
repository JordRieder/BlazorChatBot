🎃 Event: Hogsmeade Halloween Festival
🗓 Date: Friday, October 31, 2025
⏰ Time: 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM
📍 Location: Hogsmeade Village
👥 Approximate Attendance: 200 students
🧾 Description:
The annual Hogsmeade Halloween Festival invites all students in third year and above to celebrate the spooky season with a night of magical festivities.
Highlights include:
- Pumpkin pasties and other seasonal treats
- Enchanted hayrides through the village outskirts
- A costume contest judged by Professor McGonagall
Students must submit permission slips in advance to attend. The event is designed to foster community spirit and offer a safe, supervised celebration of Halloween traditions in the wizarding world.
