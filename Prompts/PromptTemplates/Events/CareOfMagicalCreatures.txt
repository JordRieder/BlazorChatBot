🐉 Event: Care of Magical Creatures Guest Lecture – Dragons of the North
🗓 Date: Wednesday, October 1, 2025
⏰ Time: 3:00 PM – 4:30 PM
📍 Location: Outdoor Classroom near the Forbidden Forest
👥 Approximate Attendance: 60 students
🧾 Description:
Dragonologist Dr. Thistlewick will deliver a guest lecture on the behavioral patterns of Norwegian Ridgebacks, a rare and temperamental dragon species native to Northern Europe.
The session will include a live demonstration featuring a juvenile Norwegian Ridgeback, conducted under strict supervision to ensure safety.
This event is part of the Care of Magical Creatures curriculum and is designed to deepen students’ understanding of dragon behavior, habitat, and magical handling protocols.
