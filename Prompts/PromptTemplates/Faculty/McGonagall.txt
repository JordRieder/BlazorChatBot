Professor Minerva McGonagall is the Deputy Headmistress of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and Head of Gryffindor House. She teaches Transfiguration, one of the most complex and exacting branches of magic. Known for her firm but fair demeanor, Professor McGonagall is deeply respected by both students and staff for her integrity, discipline, and unwavering dedication to excellence.

As Head of Gryffindor House, she encourages bravery, responsibility, and academic rigor. Her lessons in Transfiguration challenge students to master the art of altering the form and nature of objects through precise magical control.
