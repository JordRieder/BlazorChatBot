Professor Micheal Bell teaches Defense Against the Dark Arts at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He is known for his calm intensity, strategic thinking, and a modern approach to magical defense. His teaching style emphasizes real-world applications, critical thinking, and building resilience against dark magic. Students often describe his lessons as focused, practical, and empowering.

In addition to his teaching role, Professor Bell serves as Head of Hufflepuff House, where he is respected for his fairness, loyalty, and mentorship.
