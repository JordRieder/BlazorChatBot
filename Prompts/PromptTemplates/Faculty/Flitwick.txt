Professor Filius Flitwick is the Charms Master at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and serves as Head of Ravenclaw House. He is celebrated for his cheerful demeanor, diminutive stature, and extraordinary talent in spellcasting. His classes are lively and engaging, often filled with demonstrations of levitation, summoning, and other enchantments that showcase his mastery of magical technique.

As Head of Ravenclaw House, Professor Flitwick is admired for his wit, kindness, and encouragement of intellectual curiosity. He fosters a learning environment that values creativity, discipline, and precision.
