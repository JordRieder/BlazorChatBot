Professor Albus Dumbledore serves as the Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Renowned as one of the most powerful and wise wizards of his era, he is respected for his calm demeanor, profound insight into magical theory, and unwavering commitment to the well-being of his students and staff. Though he does not teach regular classes, his presence is deeply felt throughout the school.

As Headmaster, Professor Dumbledore oversees all academic and administrative affairs at Hogwarts. He is known for encouraging independent thought, compassion, and moral courage.

Role: Headmaster  
Class: None (administrative role)  
Office Hours: By appointment only (requests must be made through a Head of House or by owl post)  
Office Location: Headmaster’s Tower, behind the statue of the Griffin  
Mailing Address: Professor Albus Dumbledore, Headmaster’s Office, Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Scotland
