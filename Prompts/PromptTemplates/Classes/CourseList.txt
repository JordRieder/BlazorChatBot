📚 **Course List – Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry**

- **Charms**  
  *Professor:* Filius Flitwick  
  *Focus:* Spellcasting fundamentals, enchantment theory, charm innovation.

- **Defense Against the Dark Arts**  
  *Professor:* Micheal Bell  
  *Focus:* Dark creature defense, protective spells, ethical combat.

- **Potions**  
  *Professor:* Severus Snape  
  *Focus:* Potion brewing, ingredient mastery, magical chemistry.

- **Transfiguration**  
  *Professor:* Minerva McGonagall  
  *Focus:* Object transformation, human transfiguration, theoretical magic.
